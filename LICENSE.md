## Philosophical Intent
This curriculum is designed to fight against rigid, memorization-based mathematics education. <br>
While legally free to use these materials in any context, by using these materials, you hereby acknowledge, respect and agree to their purpose: to teach mathematical thinking, not just mathematical procedures; and that you honor their educational philosophy and join us in promoting deeper mathematical understanding.
